My Travel Gallery


Great Wall of China

Walking along the Great Wall was an unforgettable experience, every step felt like traveling back in time. The breathtaking views of the mountains reminded me how incredible human determination can be.



Irosin Hilltop Church - Bicol

Irosin Hilltop Church is one of the most peaceful and beautiful places I’ve visited. The climb going up is worth it because once you reach the top, you can see the wide view of the town and the surrounding mountains. The cool breeze and quiet atmosphere make it the perfect spot to reflect and appreciate nature’s beauty.



Venice Grand Canal - McKinley Hill, Taguig City

Venice Grand Canal at McKinley Hill is a beautiful place that feels calm and modern at the same time. I really enjoyed walking around the colorful buildings and bridges that look like a mini version of Venice.



Laiya Beach - San Juan, Batangas

Laiya Beach is a tropical escape filled with white sand, clear blue water, and peaceful waves. I loved how relaxing it felt, the perfect place to unwind and enjoy the beauty of the sea.
