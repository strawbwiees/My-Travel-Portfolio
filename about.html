About Me

Traveling has always been something that makes us feel alive. One of our favorite memories is visiting the Venice Grand Canal in McKinley Hill, where we enjoyed the peaceful scenery and colorful buildings that made us feel like I was in another country. Another unforgettable trip was to Laiya Beach in Batangas, where the white sand and clear water gave me the perfect escape from the noise of the city. I’ll never forget walking along the Great Wall of China, a place that showed me how amazing human history can be. And in Irosin Hilltop Church, standing at the top and looking down at the quiet town below made me realize how calming and beautiful simple moments can be.


Each of these places taught me something different, from adventure and curiosity to peace and reflection. That’s why I love to explore: every journey reminds me how wonderful the world is and how much there is still to see and experience.


These trips have taught us that traveling is not just about seeing new places, but also about experiencing different cultures and making memories that last a lifetime. We can’t wait to explore more of the world and share our adventures with you!
